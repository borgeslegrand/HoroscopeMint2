FAQ for HoroscopeMint miniapp on the Base network.

1. What is HoroscopeMint?

HoroscopeMint is a digital horoscope platform where you can get daily personalized horoscope readings based on your zodiac sign and mint them as NFTs on the Base network.

2. Which network does it run on?

HoroscopeMint runs entirely on the Base network, providing fast and low-cost transactions for daily check-ins and NFT minting.

3. How much is the minting fee?

Each NFT minting requires 1 USDC + gas fee. Daily check-ins are free and earn you 1 point each.

4. Which payment methods are supported?

Currently, only USDC on the Base network is supported for NFT minting. Daily check-ins are completely free.

5. How do I get daily horoscope readings?

Simply select your zodiac sign and category, then click "Check-in" to receive your personalized daily horoscope reading. You can mint it as an NFT for 1 USDC if you want to keep it permanently.

6. What categories are available?

We offer 5 categories: Love & Relationships, Money & Career, Crypto & Investment, Career & Success, and NFT & Digital Assets.

7. How does the point system work?

Daily check-ins earn you 1 point each, while minting an NFT earns you 10 points. Points are tracked in your profile and contribute to your overall ranking.

8. Is HoroscopeMint free to use?

Yes! Daily horoscope readings and check-ins are completely free. You only pay when you choose to mint your horoscope as an NFT (1 USDC + gas).
